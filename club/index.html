Education Foundation Club at BRHS

Students Helping Their Schools, One Event at a Time

The Education Foundation Club at Bridgewater-Raritan High School is a student-run group that supports the Education Foundation of Bridgewater-Raritan.

Our team includes two co-presidents and seven committee chairs across Finance, Events & Volunteering, Membership & Marketing, and Technology.

When students run events, the money raised goes right back to the Foundation and helps fund teacher grants across the district.

How The Club Runs

Each committee owns a part of the work, and together we plan events that raise money for classroom grants.

Finance

Tracks goals, budgets events, and makes sure fundraising is handled well.

Membership & Marketing

Promotes events, grows membership, and keeps students informed.

Events & Volunteering

Assists in planning events and organizes volunteers.

Technology

Handles digital tools, website updates, and other technology.

How It Helps Classrooms

1

Students Plan Events

Club members can pick ideas, set goals, and coordinate the fundraiser.

2

Community Shows Up

Families, staff, and local sponsors can support the event.

3

Funds Go to the Foundation

Event proceeds are directed to the Education Foundation of BR.

4

Teachers Receive Grants

Those funds help teachers run new projects for students.
