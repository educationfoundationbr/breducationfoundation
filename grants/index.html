Mini Grant Application

Use this form to submit a Mini Grant request. "*" indicates required fields.

Before You Submit
  • Applicants must be BRRSD faculty or staff.
  • Funding limits: up to $750 for classroom projects and up to $1,500 for collaborative projects.
  • Include the project summary, schools/students impacted, and an itemized budget.
  • Projects must align with at least one core funding area and include principal approval.
  • Applications are reviewed by trustees and district leaders; funded projects submit a short impact update with media.
